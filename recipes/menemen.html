Turkish Menemen

Description

Turkish Menemen is an egg dish that resembles scrambled eggs but it also brings in tomatoes, onions and peppers into the mixture.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Whole onion
  • 1 Whole tomato (or canned tomato puree)
  • 1 Big bell pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. First heat a pan with enough oil to coat the base
  2. Then whisk your eggs in a container of your choice until it's completely yellow throughout
  3. Afterwards, chop up the onion, chop up the bell pepper and run the tomato through a grater
  4. When the pan is hot, throw in the onions and cook until pink
  5. Then, throw in the bell peppers and cook them until they start to shrivel up
  6. Throw in the grated tomatoes and cook until the water boils away from the tomatoes
  7. Lastly, put in the whisked eggs into the pan and starting mixing vigorously to avoid clumping
  8. Finally add seasonings such as: cumin, dried pepper, black pepper, salt. Your eggs are done when you are not having trouble getting a bite with a fork but it is not completely liquid either.
