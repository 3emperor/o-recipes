Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Chicken schnitzel is a great way to eat chicken if you like deep fried chicken. It involves coating chicken in bread crumbs and cooking them in oil.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Fine bread crumbs (Panko if you can find them)
  • Spices of your choice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. First get your chicken breasts thin (1.5-2 cm thick), you can cut them in half or beat them with a mallet
  2. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper and let them sit for 30 minutes if you have the time
  3. Whisk your eggs in a container to make an egg wash
  4. Season the breadcrumbs with spices of your choice
  5. Put the oil in a pan and get it really hot
  6. Afterwards, get your chicken breasts and coat them in normal flour then in the egg wash and finally in the breadcrumbs
  7. After coating the chicken breasts in the given order, put them in the pan and fry on each side for 5 minutes minimum
  8. If the outside of the chicken starts darkening really fast, lower the heat.
