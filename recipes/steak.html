Steak

Description

Steak is a dish that is known worldwide. It involves getting a decent cut of red meat (Tenderloin in this recipe) and searing them in a pan.

Ingredients

  • Tenderloin (Ideally 2 cm thick)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Salt your tenderloin with salt and pepper
  2. Coat the base of a pan with vegetable oil and get it smoking hot
  3. Put the steak in and cook each side for 2 minutes
  4. Afterwards put the heat on low, throw in thyme, crushed garlic and butter
  5. After the butter gets bubbly and brown, baste the steak with the butter
  6. Put the steak on a cutting board and let it rest for 2 minutes
