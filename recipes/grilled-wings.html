Grilled Wings

Description

Grilled chicken wings are a delicious recipe for those who like chicken. It simply involves saucing up our wings and cooking them on a grill.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings (pre-sauced if you are lazy)
  • Vegetable oil (To coat the wings with so our spice mixture sticks)
  • Spice mixture:
    • 4 table spoons of sweet paprika
    • 2 tea spoons of grounded cumin
    • 1.5 tea spoons of grounded black pepper
    • 1.5 tea spoons of garlic powder
    • 1.5 tea spoons of onion powder
    • 1.5 tea spoons of chili flakes
    • 1.5 tea spoons of oregano or thyme
    • 1.5 tea spoons of salt
    • 1 tea spoon of sugar

Steps

  1. Get your wings in a container big enough
  2. Coat them all with vegetable oil so the spice sticks.
  3. Throw in the spice mixture and start mixing them with your hands
  4. After each wing is properly coated in the spice mixture get a barbecue or grill
  5. Heat up your grill and cook the wings until you can see the grill marks on each side.
  6. Stab the wings with a fork to see if its somewhat firm inside to know it's cooked. (4 minutes per-side)
